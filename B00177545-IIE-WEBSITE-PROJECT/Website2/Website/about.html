About Us

Celebrating Indian culture through music, dance, and entertainment in Ireland.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to bringing the best of Indian entertainment to Ireland, from Bollywood dance shows to classical music performances. Our goal is to create a vibrant and inclusive community.

Founded in 2020, Ireland Indian Entertainments has been at the forefront of promoting Indian culture through various artistic expressions. We collaborate with talented artists from both India and Ireland to create authentic and engaging experiences for our audiences.

Through our events and performances, we aim to bridge cultural gaps and foster understanding between communities, while providing top-quality entertainment that celebrates the rich heritage of Indian arts.

Our Vision

To be the leading Indian cultural hub in Ireland, offering high-quality performances and entertainment that connect people through art and tradition.

We envision a future where Indian cultural expressions are widely appreciated and integrated into Ireland's diverse cultural landscape. Our aim is to expand our reach to all major cities in Ireland, making Indian entertainment accessible to everyone.

By 2030, we plan to establish a dedicated cultural center that will serve as a permanent home for Indian arts in Ireland, complete with performance spaces, classrooms for workshops, and exhibition areas.

What We Offer

Live Events

Experience Bollywood nights, concerts, and traditional Indian performances.

Our live events feature renowned artists from India and talented local performers, creating unforgettable experiences that showcase the vibrancy and diversity of Indian entertainment. From colorful Bollywood extravaganzas to soul-stirring classical concerts, our events cater to various tastes and preferences.

Dance Classes

Learn Bharatanatyam, Kathak, and Bollywood dance from expert choreographers.

Our dance classes are designed for all skill levels, from beginners to advanced dancers. Led by professionally trained instructors, these classes offer authentic training in various Indian dance forms, helping participants develop technical skills while understanding the cultural context and significance of each dance style.

Music Shows

Enjoy live performances from talented Indian musicians and singers.

Our music shows feature a diverse range of Indian musical traditions, from classical ragas to contemporary Bollywood hits. We regularly host concerts by visiting artists from India as well as showcase local talent, providing a platform for cultural exchange and musical appreciation.
