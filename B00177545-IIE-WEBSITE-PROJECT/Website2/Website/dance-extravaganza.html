Dance Extravaganza

Dance Extravaganza

Price: €49.99 per ticket

Witness the spectacular diversity of Indian dance forms at our Dance Extravaganza! This breathtaking showcase brings together the finest dancers to present a journey through India's rich dance heritage, from classical traditions to contemporary innovations.

The performance will feature multiple dance styles including Bharatanatyam, Kathak, Odissi, Kuchipudi, and modern Bollywood choreography. Each segment will highlight the unique storytelling, rhythmic patterns, and expressive elements that make Indian dance so captivating. Our talented ensemble of dancers, adorned in authentic costumes, will bring to life the mythology, folklore, and cultural narratives of India.

The evening includes a colorful program booklet with information about each dance form and a special demonstration segment where audience members can learn basic mudras (hand gestures) and movements.

Date: 17th January 2026
Time: 7:30 PM - 10:00 PM
Venue: Galway Theatre
