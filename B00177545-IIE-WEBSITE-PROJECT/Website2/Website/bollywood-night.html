Bollywood Night

Price: €45.99 per ticket

Experience the glamour and energy of Bollywood with our spectacular Bollywood Night event! This vibrant celebration features dazzling dance performances, live music, and stunning costumes that bring the magic of Indian cinema to life. Our talented performers will take you on a journey through the evolution of Bollywood, from classic films to contemporary hits.

The event includes a welcome drink, Indian snacks, and a chance to learn some Bollywood dance moves yourself! Whether you're a longtime fan of Bollywood or new to Indian cinema, this immersive experience promises an unforgettable evening of entertainment.

Date: 28th April 2025
Time: 7:00 PM - 11:00 PM
Venue: Dublin Convention Centre
