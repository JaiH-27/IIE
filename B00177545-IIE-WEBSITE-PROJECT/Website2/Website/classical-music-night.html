Classical Music Night

Classical Music Night

Price: €55.99 per ticket

Immerse yourself in the rich traditions of Indian classical music with our enchanting Classical Music Night. This elegant evening features performances by renowned musicians who are masters of their craft, showcasing the depth and beauty of both Hindustani and Carnatic classical traditions.

The concert will include performances on traditional instruments such as the sitar, tabla, veena, and santoor, along with classical vocal renditions. Our featured artists will guide you through the intricate ragas and talas that form the foundation of Indian classical music, creating an atmosphere of tranquility and artistic excellence.

This intimate concert experience includes a program guide explaining the history and significance of each performance, as well as a post-concert reception where you can meet the artists.

Date: 15th December 2025
Time: 6:30 PM - 9:30 PM
Venue: Cork Opera House
